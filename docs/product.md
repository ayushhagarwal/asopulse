# Product principles

1. Show observations, not invented certainty.
2. Make every metric traceable to a source, time, confidence, and method version.
3. Keep the research workflow calm, fast, and keyboard accessible.
4. Prefer public APIs and user-owned infrastructure.
5. Remain independently designed and implemented.

## V1

Pulse, Discover, Watchlist, Settings, daily rank observations, movement signals, CSV export,
backup and restore, and Docker-first self-hosting.

## Deferred

Competitors, reviews, App Store Connect, Apple Ads, Google Play, teams, billing, AI writing,
notifications, and native wrappers.
